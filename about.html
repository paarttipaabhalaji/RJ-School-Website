School History

R.J Matriculation School was founded in 2007 by the munificent gesture of the eminnent philanthropist Mr. K.V.Raman Kutty in memory of his beloved daughter Late Selvi R.Jayalakshmi. The Management wanted to spearhead a movement of empowerment through education and stamp a mark in the field of education. The Management's desire is to stimulate love of learning and nurture human values among younger generation.
